Kanban Board:


How to Use:

1. Get started by clicking New Board. An ID will be generated of which you can load in the menu bar.
2. Once loaded you can add, remove, rename, tag or change the color of your items on the board.
3. Remember to save your board and remember your unique ID.
4. If you really want to you can delete the whole board by entering the ID at the top of the page.
